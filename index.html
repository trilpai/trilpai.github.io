Trilp AI was established as a solo software consulting studio between late 2022 and 2025.

During this period, it operated as an independent practice delivering end-to-end, production-grade software systems—covering architecture, backend APIs, cloud infrastructure, and modern web front-ends.

The work spanned multiple domains, including Industry 4.0 and IoT, healthcare and medical councils, financial valuation, waste management, ed-tech, logistics, and SaaS platforms serving the North American utility and energy sector. Engagements were carried out remotely with organizations across India, the United States, and Europe.

In late 2025, active consulting under the Trilp AI name was discontinued following a transition to permanent full-time employment. While ideas around structured learning and written material were explored, they were never executed. This site remains online as a personal record of that independent consulting phase.
